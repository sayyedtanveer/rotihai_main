# Roti Category – USP Marketing Guide

Roti is your **USP category**. Use these assets and names across the app and marketing.

---

## Catchy display names for Roti category

Pick one (or rotate for A/B testing):

| Name | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Roti Hai** | Strong brand tie-in, short |
| **Fresh Roti Daily** | Subscription / recurring orders |
| **Roti & Meals** | When you want to stress full meals, not just bread |
| **Home-Style Roti** | Homemade, trust, quality |
| **Daily Roti** | Simple, memorable, subscription-friendly |
| **Roti / Meal subscription** | Clear for subscription flows (nav, banners) |

**Recommendation:** Use **"Roti Hai"** or **"Fresh Roti Daily"** as the main category label; use **"Roti & Meals"** or **"Roti / Meal subscription"** in subscription/CTA copy.

---

## Image assets (where they’re used)

All paths are relative to the app origin (e.g. `https://yoursite.com/...`).

### 1. `roti-category-circle.png`
- **Use:** Category tab circles on **Home** (main category strip).
- **Also:** Category image in **CategoryMenuDrawer** (small square), and anywhere you show a circular or square category icon.
- **Suggested size:** Square (e.g. 256×256 or 512×512). Already generated.
- **In admin:** Set the **Roti** category’s **Image** URL to:
  - `/category-images/roti-category-circle.png`

### 2. `roti-category-banner.png`
- **Use:** Hero, promotional banners, landing, or any **horizontal** “Roti” or “Roti subscription” section.
- **Suggested size:** Wide (e.g. 1200×400 or 800×400). Already generated.
- **Where to use:** Hero component, promotional banner images (if you add image support), or landing page “Roti” section. Reference as `/category-images/roti-category-banner.png`.

---

## Category circle size (Home)

Category circles on Home are already set a bit larger:

- **Mobile:** 80px (`w-20 h-20`)
- **sm:** 96px (`w-24 h-24`)
- **md+:** 112px (`w-28 h-28`)

So the Roti category (and others) will appear in these larger circles when you use the images above.

---

## Quick checklist

1. **Admin → Categories:** Edit the Roti category:
   - **Name:** e.g. `Roti Hai` or `Fresh Roti Daily`
   - **Image:** `/category-images/roti-category-circle.png`
2. **Banners / Hero:** Use `/category-images/roti-category-banner.png` for any roti-focused banner or hero.
3. **Copy:** Use one of the catchy names in nav, CTAs, and subscription screens.
